Important Things to Know

üßº Cleaning Methods

Hot Water Extraction (Steam Cleaning): Most effective deep cleaning method. Uses hot water and cleaning solution, then extracts dirt. Takes 6-12 hours to dry. Dry Cleaning: Low-moisture method using compounds. Faster drying (2-4 hours) but less deep cleaning power.

üìÖ How Often to Clean

High-traffic areas: Every 6-12 months. Normal traffic: Every 12-18 months. Light traffic: Every 18-24 months. With pets or kids: Every 6 months. Regular cleaning extends carpet life significantly.

‚è∞ Drying Time

Steam cleaning takes 6-12 hours to dry completely. Run fans, open windows, and avoid walking on carpets in socks. Summer cleaning dries faster than winter. Plan to stay off carpets for at least 6 hours after cleaning.

Pet accidents require enzyme treatments to fully remove odors. Regular cleaning alone won't eliminate the smell. Old pet stains may have soaked into padding‚Äîsometimes padding replacement is necessary for complete odor removal.
